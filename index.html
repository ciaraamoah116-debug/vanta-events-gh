Our Story

Vanta Events GH was founded by Miss Ciara Gyamfua Amoah with a passion for transforming creative ideas into beautifully curated experiences.

We specialise in event design and styling, creating unique concepts that reflect the vision and personality of every client. Our goal is to deliver elegant, memorable experiences through creativity, organisation, and attention to detail.

For selected portfolio-building projects, Vanta Events GH offers complimentary services to showcase creativity and build meaningful experiences. Clients who book our services may also receive complimentary card design, with only the production costs covered by the client.

Event Staffing

At Vanta Events GH, we provide reliable and professional event staffing solutions. We recruit individuals, train them, and prepare them with the skills needed to support successful events.

Punctuality, professionalism, and commitment are at the heart of our staffing team. We only recruit serious individuals who are ready to learn, work responsibly, and commit to delivering excellent service.
